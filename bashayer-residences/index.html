The Project

A Distinguished Golf Address

Bashayer Residences forms part of Hudayriyat Golf Estates, a distinguished residential precinct set alongside sweeping fairways and serene water channels on Hudayriyat Island. Designed for those who value tranquility and prestige, the community brings together Andalusian-inspired architecture, pristine nature, and world-class amenities in balanced measure.

Anchored by an 18-hole golf course, a country clubhouse, and a golf academy, the estate is a place where daily life and leisure move at the same unhurried pace. Cycling trails, parks, and generous green spaces thread the neighbourhood together, while dining, retail, and wellness venues place everything essential within reach.

Location

Perfectly Connected

Bashayer Residences sits within Hudayriyat West, part of a masterplan that unites the 18-hole golf course, a sports village, retail, and the clubhouse. Neighbouring landmarks include Surf Abu Dhabi, the Velodrome, Olympia Resort Beach, Biotopia, and an equestrian centre.

The location keeps you effortlessly connected to Abu Dhabi's most important destinations, from cultural institutions to the international airport, all within a comfortable drive.

  • Zayed International Airport — 33 minutes
  • ADGM Financial Centre — 29 minutes
  • Louvre Museum — 31 minutes
  • Disneyland — 37 minutes
  • Surf Abu Dhabi & Velodrome nearby
  • Private school and community retail on site
