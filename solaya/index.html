The Project

Masterfully Crafted Beachfront Residences

Solaya is a private collection of 234 distinguished residences where thoughtfully crafted spaces exude a quiet elegance. Developed in collaboration between Meraas and Brookfield Properties, and designed by Foster + Partners and 1508 London, Solaya is set to become the city's most sought-after residential address.

Nine beachfront buildings seamlessly integrate architecture and nature, deliberately maximising their sea, city and beach views. Open-plan layouts, light-filled interiors and inviting indoor-outdoor spaces create an elegant canvas for contemporary coastal living, on your terms.

Curated gardens and shaded areas connect the community to its central Beach Park — an alfresco sanctuary designed for sports, leisure and relaxation, next to renowned hotels, beach clubs and restaurants.

Location

Perfectly Connected

Solaya is positioned along Jumeirah's coastline, a cultured coastal community with direct beach access and a privileged beachfront setting. Neighbouring J1 Beach captures the timeless allure of Riviera living, with thirteen sophisticated restaurants and beach clubs including Sirene Beach by Gaia, Gigi Rigolatto and Bâoli.

Just minutes from the dynamic heart of Dubai, the community offers effortless connectivity to the city's key destinations while remaining a secluded, gated enclave.

  • Downtown Dubai – 15-minute drive
  • Dubai International Airport – approx. 20 minutes by car
  • J1 Beach – neighbouring beachfront destination
  • The Dubai Mall & Burj Khalifa
  • DIFC & City Walk
  • Mandarin Oriental, Bvlgari Resort and Four Seasons Resort nearby

Developer

Built By The Best

Solaya is a collaboration between Meraas and Brookfield Properties. Meraas is committed to strengthening Dubai's global position in real estate through a diverse portfolio of master developments in iconic destinations, delivering exceptional residential experiences tailored to a global clientele. Brookfield Properties is a leading global operator and developer of high-quality real estate, managing more than 1,100 properties and over 395 million square feet on behalf of Brookfield Asset Management.

Architecture is by Foster + Partners, the global studio for sustainable architecture founded by Norman Foster in 1967, with bespoke interiors by 1508 London, an international design firm renowned for timeless, culturally rooted luxury spaces.
